About Great Lakes Peace Centre

Founded in 2010, the Great Lakes Peace Centre is a non-profit organization based in Kasese, Uganda, dedicated to promoting peace, reconciliation, and sustainable development in the Great Lakes region.

Our work focuses on conflict resolution, community dialogue, youth empowerment, and women's leadership development to create resilient communities where everyone can thrive.

Through our innovative programs and partnerships, we've impacted over 50,000 individuals across 30 communities in the Kasese district.

Our Program Areas / Thematic Areas

GLPC implements targeted programs that address the root causes of conflict while fostering social cohesion, health, and sustainable development across the Kasese region and the greater Rwenzori sub-region.

Conflict Prevention and Management

GLPC engages communities, youth, and local leaders in conflict resolution, mediation, and peace education. Through community dialogues and youth mentorship, the program promotes tolerance, mutual understanding, and non-violent approaches to conflict transformation.

Learn More

Peace Building

This program strengthens grassroots peace initiatives by empowering communities to reconcile differences, rebuild trust, and foster inclusive governance. It emphasizes dialogue, trauma healing, and the promotion of human rights and coexistence among diverse groups.

Learn More

Health Promotion and Socio-Economic Empowerment

GLPC enhances community well-being by promoting public health awareness, preventive healthcare, and women’s reproductive rights. The program also supports income-generating activities and skills development to improve livelihoods and reduce poverty-related vulnerabilities.

Learn More

Institutional Capacity Development of GLPC

This program focuses on strengthening GLPC’s internal systems, governance structures, staff competencies, and resource mobilization capacity. It ensures the organization remains transparent, accountable, and sustainable in delivering high-impact community programs.

Learn More

Relief and Emergency Support

GLPC responds to humanitarian crises such as floods, conflicts, and disease outbreaks by providing emergency aid, psychosocial support, and recovery assistance. The program works with partners to restore dignity and resilience among affected communities.
