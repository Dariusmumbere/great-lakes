Key Impact Areas

Our work focuses on several critical areas where we've made significant progress

Youth Empowerment

Engaging young people as agents of peace through skills training, leadership development, and conflict resolution education.

Target: 5,000 youth Achieved: 4,250

Women's Leadership

Empowering women to participate in decision-making processes and lead peace initiatives in their communities.

Target: 1,500 women Achieved: 1,350

Community Dialogue

Facilitating inter-community dialogues to resolve conflicts and build sustainable relationships.

Target: 200 dialogues Achieved: 156

Our Journey of Impact

Key milestones in our peacebuilding efforts over the years

2012

First Community Peace Agreement

Facilitated our first major peace agreement between two conflicting communities in the Rwenzori region, setting a precedent for future work.

5,000 people impacted
2 communities reconciled
2015

Youth Peace Ambassadors Program Launch

Launched our flagship youth program, training young people as peace ambassadors in their communities.

500 youth trained
15 schools involved
2017

Women's Peace Network Expansion

Expanded our women's leadership program to cover the entire Kasese district, significantly increasing women's participation in peace processes.

800 women leaders
40 peace committees formed
2020

Digital Peace Platform Launch

Introduced technology solutions to enhance our peacebuilding efforts, including a mobile app for conflict reporting and resolution.

2,000+ app users
150 conflicts reported
2023

Regional Partnership Initiative

Established partnerships with organizations across the Great Lakes region to expand our impact beyond Kasese district.

5 new partner organizations
3 new regions reached
