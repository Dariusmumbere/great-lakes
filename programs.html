Our Programs

Discover how GLPC implements targeted programs that address the root causes of conflict while fostering social cohesion, health, and sustainable development across the Kasese region.

Our Approach to Peacebuilding

At Great Lakes Peace Centre, we believe that sustainable peace requires a holistic approach that addresses the interconnected challenges facing communities in the Kasese region and the greater Rwenzori sub-region.

Our programs are designed to tackle the root causes of conflict while creating opportunities for growth, resilience, and empowerment. Through strategic partnerships and community-led initiatives, we work to build a future where all people can thrive in peace and dignity.

Each of our programs is carefully designed to complement the others, creating a comprehensive framework for sustainable development and lasting peace.

Our Program Areas

Explore our three core programs that work together to build resilient, peaceful communities.

Health Promotion Program

GLPC enhances community well-being by promoting public health awareness and preventive healthcare through various projects. Our health initiatives focus on creating healthier communities as a foundation for sustainable peace and development.

Key Initiatives:

  • Community health education and awareness campaigns
  • Preventive healthcare programs and screenings
  • Hygiene and sanitation improvement projects
  • Maternal and child health support
  • HIV/AIDS prevention and support services
Learn More

Social Development and Peace Building Program

This program focuses on empowering youth, promoting peace, and improving access to education and literacy within communities. It aims to nurture responsible, informed, and peaceful citizens who contribute to social harmony and development.

Key Initiatives:

  • Youth empowerment and leadership training
  • Peace education and conflict resolution workshops
  • Educational support and literacy programs
  • Community dialogue and reconciliation forums
  • Inter-ethnic and cross-cultural exchange programs
Learn More

FAUNA COFFEE [Social Enterprise]

Serves as a sustainable social business model that supports GLPC's mission of peacebuilding, community empowerment, and youth livelihood development. Through coffee value chain and trade, the enterprise generates income to sustain GLPC programs, while also creating employment and promoting economic resilience.

Key Initiatives:

  • Sustainable coffee farming and value chain development
  • Youth employment and skills training in agribusiness
  • Income generation to support GLPC peacebuilding programs
  • Market access for local coffee farmers
  • Environmental conservation through sustainable practices
