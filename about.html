Our Mission & Vision

The Great Lakes Peace Centre is dedicated to fostering sustainable peace, reconciliation, and development in the Kasese region and throughout the Great Lakes area of Africa.

Founded in 2010, our organization emerged from the urgent need to address ongoing conflicts and build resilient communities where every individual can thrive in dignity and security.

We believe that sustainable peace is achieved through inclusive dialogue, community empowerment, and addressing the root causes of conflict.

Our Approach

Our Values

These core principles guide everything we do at the Great Lakes Peace Centre

Reconciliation

We believe in the power of forgiveness and dialogue to heal divisions and build bridges between conflicting parties.

Inclusivity

Every voice matters. We ensure all community members, especially marginalized groups, participate in peace processes.

Integrity

We maintain transparency, accountability, and ethical standards in all our operations and relationships.

Sustainability

Our solutions are designed to create lasting change that continues to benefit communities long after our direct involvement.

Our History

Key milestones in our journey of building peace in Kasese

2010

Foundation

The Great Lakes Peace Centre was established in response to escalating conflicts in the Kasese region.

2012

First Community Dialogues

We facilitated our first inter-community dialogues, bringing together conflicting groups for peaceful resolution.

2015

Youth Empowerment Program Launch

Recognizing the importance of engaging youth, we launched our flagship youth empowerment initiative.

2018

Expansion to Rural Areas

We expanded our operations to reach remote communities in the Rwenzori mountains.

2021

Women's Leadership Initiative

Launched a dedicated program to empower women as peacebuilders in their communities.

2023

Digital Peace Platform

Introduced technology-based solutions to enhance our peacebuilding efforts across the region.
