Sustainable Peace Through Coffee

Fauna Coffee serves as a sustainable social business model that supports GLPC's mission of peacebuilding, community empowerment, and youth livelihood development.

Through coffee value chain and trade, the enterprise generates income to sustain GLPC programs, while also creating employment and promoting economic resilience among communities in the Great Lakes region.

By connecting local coffee farmers to markets and providing training in coffee production and barista skills, we're building economic opportunities that contribute to lasting peace and stability.

Our Strategic Objectives

Fauna Coffee is designed to create sustainable impact through multiple strategic objectives

Financial Sustainability

Reduce donor dependence for running secretariat costs and create a reliable source of income from local business sales.

Long-term Strategy

Implement a long-term financing strategy incorporated into the Resource Mobilization strategy.

Brand Visibility

Enhance visibility and branding of the Social venture to increase market presence and impact.

Local Consumption

Improve local consumption of coffee at affordable prices while promoting Ugandan coffee culture.

Strategic Partnerships

Develop improved strategic partnerships for coffee value addition support and marketing.

Community Empowerment

Create employment opportunities and promote economic resilience among communities in the Great Lakes region.

Harvesting

Processing

Roasting

Developing unique roast profiles for local tastes

Brewing

Training baristas in specialty coffee preparation
