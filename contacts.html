Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our work and how to get involved

How can I volunteer with the Great Lakes Peace Centre? +
We welcome volunteers in various capacities. Please contact us on our website.. We'll match your skills with our current needs.
How can my organization partner with you? +
We value partnerships with organizations that share our vision. Please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@greatlakespeace.org to discuss potential collaboration opportunities, including program implementation, funding, or technical assistance.
Do you offer internships or fellowships? +
Yes, we offer internship opportunities for students and recent graduates interested in peacebuilding. Our fellowship program is available for experienced professionals. Check our Careers page for current openings and application deadlines.
